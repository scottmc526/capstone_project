Strike (X) = 10 plus the next two balls

Example: Frame 1 = X, Frame 2 = 8, 1

Frame One Total = 10 + 8 + 1 = 19

Another Example: Frame 1 = X, Frame 2 = X, Frame 3 = X

Frame One Total = 10 + 10 + 10 = 30 (This is why the maximum possible score is 300)


Spare ( / ) = 10 Plus the next shot

Example: Frame 1 = 9 / , Frame 2 = 8 1

Frame One Total = 10 + 8 = 18 (the second shot doesn't count for the spare)

Yet Another Example: Frame 1 = 9 /, Frame 2 = X

Frame One Total = 10 + 10 = 20


When you enter your score in this app, it shows you the total for that frame instead of your overall total to help understand the scoring system!
